Matibabu by MaishaKenya

We Offer
Online Medical Consultation

Matibabu is a platform where patients are able to receive medical consultation from qualified doctors virtually from any location

Inorder to use this service, there is a one-time consultation fee of Ksh 250. To get continue, click on 'Consult A Doctor' below.

Schedule a session

You can request to see a doctor for a 15 minute virtual consultation whenever works best for you. We're open from 8am to 11pm, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. We guarantee that you'll always be able to see a doctor same-day. Right care when you need it most.

Talk to a doctor

Securely and privately have your consultation wherever you see fit. You can use your computer or tablet. Once your consultation is complete, you can immediately access your doctor's treatment notes, download your prescription, and more through your dashboard.

Get treated

Our doctors can provide medical advice, diagnose, treat, prescribe and even refer you onto a specialist. If you need a prescription, it will be instantly sent to your inbox to take to your chosen pharmacy or you can have it fufilled & delivered via MYDAWA.
